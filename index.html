Table Rock Vacation Home Rentals

Welcome to Table Rock

We are located in the beautiful upstate of South Carolina near Table Rock State Park. The area has an abundance of beautiful, breathtaking waterfalls and is surrounded by the Jocassee Gorges Nature Preserve with over 185,000 pristine acres under conservation management. Wildlife abounds, with Wild Turkey, Wild Boar, Black Bear, Deer, Coyote, Fox and a large variety of birds. Visit the area once and you will not want to live anywhere else.

Who We Are

We are Table Rock Vacation Home Rentals and we specialize in renting Homes in and around the Rock at Jocassee Golf Community. We are also residents of the community. The Community offers a diversity in Architectural styles from Log Homes overlooking Fairways on the Golf Course to Stylish Round Homes with Mountain views and to Conventional Homes with Unconventional Turret Tower on Mountain tops.

Vacation Rentals

The Rock at Jocassee Golf Resort has been a favorite destination for regional golfers and vacationers for 25 years. Located in the heart of upcountry South Carolina along the Cherokee Foothills Scenic Highway 11, this resort is situated just a few miles from the entrance to Table Rock State Park. Enjoy hiking, bicycling, horseback riding, swimming, tennis or mountain sightseeing while visiting the resort. Play our masterfully designed and beautifully maintained 18 hole, 6800 yard, par 72 golf course amidst the backdrop of scenic views, water falls and mountain streams.
